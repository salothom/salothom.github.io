About

Hi! My name is Sarah and am a software developer based in Austin, Texas. Originally from the Chicago suburbs, I have strong opinions on Italian Beefs and snow days.

I gradutated from Indiana Univeristy in 2016 with a degree in Informatics from the School of Informatics and Computer Science.At IU I worked at their award winning student radio station WIUX and an undergraduate instructor for an Intro to Python course.

After graduating I moved down to the Lone Star State, and now I spend most of my free time trying to learn new tech skills, crafting, captaining a city league kickball team, and faciliting the book club I founded.

Projects

Book Club and Music New Years Resolution

I made this site to keep track of the books that the book club I founded has read, for ease of access and reference when ever we meet and select a new book. It is also used to keep track of my 2020 new years resolution to listen to 100 albums with "fresh ears". This site is written in Angular 7, and hosted via github pages. All of the book and album data is stored in a json.
